The Brief

R Samdani Advisory required a complete brand overhaul to match their expanding global footprint. The challenge was to create an identity that felt deeply established and trustworthy, yet thoroughly modern and adaptable to digital platforms.

We stripped back the visual noise, focusing on absolute typographic clarity and a monochromatic palette punctuated by stark architectural lines.
